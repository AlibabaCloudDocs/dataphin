# Unpublish or delete business filters {#concept_p3w_3h4_fhb .concept}

This topic describes how to unpublish and delete a business filter.

1.  Choose **R&D** \> **Develop** \> **Standards and Modeling** \> **Business Filters** to open the corresponding page.
2.  In the left-side navigation pane, select the business filter to be unpublished or deleted, click the More icon ![](http://static-aliyun-doc.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/assets/img/149433/156134677641498_en-US.png), and then select **Unpublish** or **Delete**.

    **Note:** Before you unpublish or delete a business filter, you need to ensure that the business filter is not referenced by any derived metrics.
